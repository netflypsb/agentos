# 🚀 AgentOS Development Roadmap

## Executive Summary

AgentOS is a unified MCP server providing essential operating system services for AI agents. This roadmap outlines a 3-phase development plan designed for rapid market entry and sustainable monetization.

**Timeline**: 12 weeks to MVP, 24 weeks to full commercial release
**Target Market**: AI agent developers using Claude Desktop, Cursor, Windsurf, OpenClaw, and custom MCP clients
**Revenue Model**: Freemium → $15/mo subscription via MCP Marketplace

---

## Phase Overview

| Phase | Duration | Focus | Key Deliverables | Revenue Target |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Phase 1** | Weeks 1-4 | Core MVP Development | 3 essential tools, basic MCP server, free tier | $0 (user acquisition) |
| **Phase 2** | Weeks 5-8 | Commercial Release | All 6 tools, monetization, marketplace launch | $2,250 MRR |
| **Phase 3** | Weeks 9-24 | Scale & Expand | Enterprise features,生态系统, advanced tools | $10,000+ MRR |

## Risk Mitigation Strategy

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Technical Complexity** | Medium | High | Start with 3 simplest tools, incremental complexity |
| **Market Competition** | Low | Medium | First-mover advantage, single-install value prop |
| **MCP Marketplace Changes** | Low | High | Diversify distribution (npm, GitHub, direct) |
| **Security Vulnerabilities** | Low | High | Zero external deps, minimal attack surface |
| **User Adoption** | Medium | High | Freemium model, essential pain point focus |

## Resource Requirements

### Phase 1: Solo Developer
- **Time**: 20-30 hours/week
- **Cost**: $0 (open source tools, free services)
- **Skills**: TypeScript, MCP SDK, SQLite, API design

### Phase 2: Small Team
- **Team**: 1-2 developers, 1 community manager
- **Budget**: $500-1,000/month (hosting, marketing)
- **Tools**: CI/CD, analytics, customer support platform

### Phase 3: Growth Team
- **Team**: 2-3 developers, 1 support, 1 marketing
- **Budget**: $3,000-5,000/month (infrastructure, growth)
- **Infrastructure**: Enterprise hosting, advanced analytics

## Decision Points & Go/No-Go Criteria

### End of Phase 1 Decision Point
**Go Criteria**:
- 100+ GitHub stars
- 50+ active Discord members
- Successful integration with 3+ major MCP clients
- <5 critical bugs reported

**No-Go Triggers**:
- <20 installations after 4 weeks
- Major technical blockers discovered
- Competitor launches similar solution

### End of Phase 2 Decision Point
**Go Criteria**:
- $1,000+ MRR achieved
- 80%+ user satisfaction rating
- <48h support response time maintained

**No-Go Triggers**:
- <50 paid conversions after 8 weeks
- >30% monthly churn rate
- Major security incidents
